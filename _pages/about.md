My research focuses on NLP, in particular building architectures to prevent massive resource consumption and algorithmic misbehavior. I also share my knowledge by teaching master's students in media programming at HTW Berlin. I am particularly interested in AI on societal problems, such as health, education, climate change, social justice, and poverty.

Research areas: Commonsense knowledge & reasoning, NLU, Neuro-symbolic AI, Green AI, Human-centric AI.

I have been working in software engineering and IT consulting for more than 13 years.

Feel free to contact me at: sascha.melcher@uni-potsdam.de
