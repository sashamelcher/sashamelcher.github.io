This paper analyses the topics of mobile learning to discuss essential cognitive aspects, developing and pushing the vision of an efficient mobile learning environment. It describes the components of language learning to enable the benefits of mobile technology for learning vocabulary. It also describes the concept and implementation of a mobile vocabulary trainer application on the Android platform.
